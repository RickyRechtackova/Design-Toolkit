Color Palette Generator

A color palette generator is a tool designed to assist designers in creating cohesive and visually appealing color schemes for their projects. Using principles of color theory, the generator generates a set of colors that complement each other well and form a harmonious palette. After colors are generated, user can click on them and the color code will automatically copy into their clipboard.

Colour Contrast Checker

Colour contrast checking is crucial for designers as it ensures readability and accessibility for all users, particularly those with visual impairments, enhancing the overall user experience.
Unsure of whether your colours have a suitable level of contrast? Try our checker below!

What your result means:

AAA

Well done!

This means you have achieved the highest level of accessibility according to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). Meeting this AAA standard indicates that the colour contrast between text and its background is sufficient for users with the most significant visual impairments, ensuring optimal readability and accessibility.

AA

That's Okay!

This means you have achieved a level of accessibility according to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). Meeting the AA standard indicates that the colour contrast between text and its background is sufficient for most users, including those with mild to moderate visual impairments, ensuring readability and accessibility for a broader range of individuals.

Fail

Uh Oh!

This means that the colour contrast between text and its background does not meet the minimum accessibility standards outlined in the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). This means that the text may be difficult to read for many users, particularly those with visual impairments, potentially leading to a poor user experience and reduced accessibility.
